## About Section

### Who We Are
Our beloved ancestor, the Late Abdul Sattar Khan, started his legal practice in Pilibhit district of Uttar Pradesh, approximately 50 years ago, after graduating from Lucknow University. His legacy is now being successfully carried forward by his daughter, Ms. Benazir Khan (B.Sc., LL.B.), and his nephew, Mr. Saleem Khan (B.Com., LL.B).

So, as a reliable law firm, with over 10 professionals with diverse skills and experience in intricate, high-profile, and path-breaking engagements. Team of highly motivated partners, lawyers, and professionals delivering exceptional client service and expert legal advice across a wide gamut of sectors and industries.

We take pride in our approach to work, which focuses on understanding clients' businesses and anticipating their legal needs, and provides pragmatic and commercial solutions.

### Our Vision
Our vision is to be one of the most progressive and innovative law firms. We instinctively take a fresh perspective on situations, exploring whether there are newer and better ways of delivering practical, commercial solutions to the challenges our clients face in today's rapidly changing business landscape.

## Practice Areas

### 1. Intellectual Property
"IP assets—ranging from proprietary concepts to established brands—are increasingly vulnerable to sophisticated infringement tactics. As these threats grow in complexity and scale, the need for robust safeguarding strategies becomes critical to maintaining market exclusivity and brand integrity."

### 2. Banking & Finance
Our banking and finance practice team has wide experience across various streams of the sector. They come from diverse backgrounds and qualifications, including working with in-house at legal teams within leading establishments. This versatility of work helps us serve our clients from diverse backgrounds and needs. Overall, we counsel all the banking & finance-related issues.

### 3. Commercial Issues
We advise institutions and individuals on a wide range of complex corporate and commercial matters. Working with our clients on such matters gives us valuable insight into their businesses, which in turn enhances the services we provide in transactional or litigation matters. We provide proactive solutions from the outset, allowing our clients to focus on their core business activities while we handle their commercial issues.

### 4. Dispute Resolution
Our dispute resolution practice offers services spread across litigation, arbitration, regulatory, and pre-dispute advisory assignments. We actively represent clients on a gamut of litigations in India before various forums such as the Court, Tribunals, Appellate, and Regulatory bodies. Our robust team advises clients on complex domestic and international commercial arbitration matters.

### 5. Real Estate & Construction
Legal Khan Associates provides a full range of corporate Real Estate services to its clients. Corporations, developers and individuals have consistently retained our highly experienced team for its advice at all stages of real estate transactions. The Real Estate Practice Group is proficient in handling a variety of matters requirement basis.

### 6. Family Law
Divorce & Annulment, Legal Separation, Prenuptial & Postnuptial Agreements, Domestic Partnerships, Child-Related Cases- Child Custody & Visitation, Child Support, Paternity/Parentage, Adoption & Surrogacy, Termination of Parental Rights, Domestic Safety and Support- Domestic Violence Injunctions, Alimony (Spousal Support), Dependency & Neglect, Guardianship etc.

### 7. GST
"Success in business begins with a seamless, legally sound setup. We are a leading firm specializing in tax and business solutions that protect your company from future regulatory issues. Our approach involves personalized consultations to clarify necessary documentation and legal mandates, ensuring your operations remain uninterrupted. Let us handle the complexities of compliance so you can focus on growth."

#### GST Services:
- **GST Registration**: Only GST Registration for your business
- **GST Annual Package**: Full Fledged GST related annual package for your business. This package includes GST Registration and 12 month GSTR (non-consulting) including ITR
- **GST+ Business Premium Package**: Full Fledged GST related annual package for your business. This package includes GST Registration and 12 month GSTR (with-consulting) including ITR (with consultation)
